Welcome to the PetaVerse

The PetaVerse is an utility based collection of 10,000 NFTs, with each NFT being randomly generated by hand-drawn artwork providing the highest quality JPEG on the Ethereum blockchain.
​
​Buying a unique NFT will gain you exclusive access to the 'Kennels': a luxury residence for your pet. By participating and also beating our Play to Win game you can ensure your pet has the best home available to them as you will be awarded items and decorations that will be automatically placed in your kennel.

The kennels are a unique place for your pets to live in luxury. These come in all shapes and sizes depending on your pet, and can be accessed through the kennels tab. To access the game you need to own a pet. You will be able earn items for you kennel by participating in our upcoming play-to-win game. More details will be released after the mint!
