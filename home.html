Welcome to the PetaVerse

The PetaVerse is an utility based collection of 10,000 NFTs, with each NFT being randomly generated by hand-drawn artwork providing the highest quality JPEG on the Ethereum blockchain.

Buying a unique NFT will gain you exclusive access to the 'Kennels': a luxury residence for your pet. By participating and also beating our Play to Win game you can ensure your pet has the best home available to them as you will be rewarded items and decorations that will be automatically placed in your kennel.

The Kennels and The Pet Arcade

Welcome to the most luxurious home on the Ethereum blockchain. after owning pets of our own and understand you would treat them the same as a family member, we decided to bring that to reality and give each owner of one of our NFTS their very own 'Kennel' - however these aren't your average kennel that you would find in your local pet store.
